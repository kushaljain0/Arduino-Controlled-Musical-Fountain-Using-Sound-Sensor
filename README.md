## List of Components -->
1. Arduino UNO Microcontroller 
2. LM393 Sound Sensor
3. 1 Channel 5 V Relay Module 
4. 12 V DC Pump 
5. 3.3V/5V Breadboard Power Supply Module 
6. LED
7. Resistors (220 ohm) 
8. Breadboard 
9. 12 V Power Supply
